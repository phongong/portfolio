This Tableau dashboard is a financial summary tool designed to provide leadership with a clear and dynamic view of current and historical weekly financial data. It includes key metrics such as Total Accounts Receivable (AR), scheduled payments, balances, paid and unpaid invoices, credit memos, and unapplied cash. The dashboard features interactive toggles and filters that allow users to adjust the date range and scope of data, enabling a tailored analysis of financial trends. By visualizing the movement of payments and highlighting unpaid invoices, the dashboard helps leadership identify and address outstanding invoices before they age, ensuring better cash flow management and financial oversight. The tool is instrumental in tracking financial health and making data-driven decisions to optimize revenue collection.
